CEREMONY DETAILS

Sky Rooftop Gazebo
Location
Sky Rooftop Gazebo – 10th floor, highest point of the resort with panoramic Caribbean views.
How to Get There
From the main lobby: Take the elevators to the 10th floor. Follow signs to the Sky Rooftop Gazebo (next to Sky Salon).
From pools or beach areas: Ask any staff member – they’ll point you to the rooftop elevators or direct you. It’s the top level with the best ocean views.
Allow 5–10 minutes from most areas. Arrive by 4:50 PM.
Seating & Etiquette
  • Open seating
  • Please silence phones – no pictures or videos during the ceremony (professional photos will be shared later)
  • Cocktail hour begins right after the ceremony (~5:30 PM)
Rooftop Tips
  • August is hot and humid (85–90°F / 29–32°C). Brief showers possible but usually quick.
  • Sunset views are beautiful around 7:00–7:30 PM.
  • It can be sunny and breezy – sunscreen and sunglasses recommended.
  • Light layer for after sunset.
  • Comfortable shoes (some walking involved).
After the Reception
Reception ends at 9:30 PM. We’ll be hanging out at the bars around the resort — feel free to join us!
Need Help?
Speak to the concierge or any staff member – they have all the wedding details.

THE RESORT

Amenities & Facilities
Pools & Beach
7+ heated pools including large lagoon pool (10ft deep), 3 man-made cenote-style pools, rooftop infinity pools (Preferred Club), multiple swim-up bars, and pristine white-sand beach with calm Caribbean waters.
Secrets Spa by Pevonia
16 treatment rooms + 5 VIP double rooms. Hydrotherapy circuit (hot/cold stations), sauna, steam room, couples massages, outdoor hydro pool with jets and waterfalls.
Fitness & Sports
Modern fitness center, tennis court, pickleball courts, archery, kayaking, golf simulator, billiards.
Entertainment & Bars
Theater with nightly shows, Gypsy Club speakeasy, sports bar, 8–9 bars total (including swim-up), cocktail classes, live music.
Other Highlights
24-hour room service, shops, Wine Boutique, Preferred Club rooftop lounge & private pool, lush gardens & cenotes throughout the property.
All-Inclusive Perks
Unlimited gourmet dining & premium drinks, WiFi, non-motorized water sports, daily activities, and entertainment.
