Wedding Location

Sky Rooftop Gazebo & Directions

Ceremony Location

Sky Rooftop Gazebo on the 10th floor of Secrets Moxché, at the highest point of the resort near Sky Salon.

From the Main Lobby

Take the main lobby elevators to the 10th floor, then follow signs toward the Sky Rooftop Gazebo and Sky Salon.

From the Pools or Beach

Head toward the main lobby elevator area. Any resort staff member can direct you to the rooftop elevators.

When to Leave

Allow 5–10 minutes from most areas of the resort and arrive by 4:50 PM. The ceremony begins promptly at 5:00 PM.

Need help? Ask the concierge or any resort staff member for “Armin and Christina’s wedding at the Sky Rooftop Gazebo.”

Dress Code

Resort formal / cocktail attire. Neutral, soft, and muted colors are encouraged. Comfortable shoes are recommended.

Unplugged Ceremony

Please silence and put away phones and cameras so everyone can be fully present. Professional photos will be shared afterward.

Your All-Inclusive Stay

What’s Included at the Resort

Most of the resort is included with your stay. Your room category determines access to Preferred Club and Impression-only areas.

All Secrets Moxché Guests Included
  • Unlimited dining: Gourmet restaurants, buffet, café, grill and 24-hour in-room dining. Standard restaurant reservations are generally not required.
  • Unlimited drinks: Domestic and international top-shelf spirits, cocktails, juices, soft drinks and house selections.
  • Pool and beach service: Food-and-drink service at designated pool and beach areas, plus access to the main pools, beach and cenote-style pools.
  • Service around the clock: 24-hour room service and concierge assistance, plus an in-room refreshment bar serviced according to your room category.
  • Wi-Fi, taxes and gratuities: Resort Wi-Fi is included, and Hyatt states that standard taxes and gratuities are included.
  • Activities and fitness: Fitness center, yoga, dance lessons, scheduled daytime activities and selected non-motorized water activities such as kayaking or snorkeling, subject to weather and the daily program.
  • Nightlife and entertainment: Live music, evening shows, karaoke, theater programming, resort bars and the Gypsy music lounge/speakeasy when scheduled.
  • Dietary options: Vegetarian and gluten-free choices are available. Tell the restaurant host about allergies or dietary needs.
  • Sip, Savor & See: Eligible guests may arrange a complimentary dinner, drinks and entertainment at a nearby participating resort through the concierge; advance arrangements and terms apply.
Preferred Club Guests Additional access
  • Exclusive Preferred Club rooftop pool and rooftop restaurant, along with enhanced room amenities and Preferred Club privileges.
Impression Guests Additional access
  • Dedicated butler service, access to Impression-only pools and restaurants, and elevated in-suite amenities and service. Confirm the exact current benefits for your room with your butler.
Usually Extra Additional charge
  • Spa and salon treatments, private or romantic dinners, premium bottles and wines, excursions, golf, motorized activities and selected culinary experiences such as tasting menus or Omakase.
Best same-day source: Resort hours, activities, inclusions and access can change. Check the Hyatt app or ask the concierge before making plans.
Useful While You’re Here

Guest Essentials
  • World of Hyatt app: Check restaurant hours, menus, daily activities, entertainment, spa information, and resort updates.
  • Concierge: Dial 0 from your room or visit the lobby for dining, spa, directions, or wedding-location help.
  • Evening dining: Keep one resort-elegant outfit ready for à la carte restaurants.
  • Small cash: The stay is all-inclusive, but small bills are useful for optional gratuities.
  • August weather: Expect heat, humidity, strong sun, and occasional brief showers. Carry sunscreen and a light layer.
  • Same-day information: Resort hours and access rules can change, so the concierge and app are the best sources during the stay.
