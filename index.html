How to Get There

From the main lobby, take the elevators to the 10th floor and follow signs for the Sky Rooftop Gazebo. Resort staff can point you to the correct elevators.

Useful While You’re Here

Guest Essentials
  • World of Hyatt app: Check restaurant hours, menus, daily activities, entertainment, spa information, and resort updates.
  • Concierge: Dial 0 from your room or visit the lobby for dining, spa, directions, or wedding-location help.
  • Evening dress code: Keep one resort-elegant outfit ready for à la carte restaurants.
  • Small cash: The stay is all-inclusive, but small bills are useful for optional gratuities.
  • August weather: Expect heat, humidity, strong sun, and occasional brief showers. Carry sunscreen and a light layer.
  • Plans can change: Resort hours and access rules may shift during the week, so the concierge and app are the best sources for same-day information.
