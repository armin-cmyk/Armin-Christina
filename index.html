Ceremony & Attire

What Guests Need to Know

Getting There

Take the main lobby elevators to the 10th floor and follow signs to the Sky Rooftop Gazebo. Allow 5–10 minutes from most resort areas.

Dress Code

Resort formal / cocktail attire. Neutral, soft, and muted colors are encouraged. Comfortable shoes are recommended.

Unplugged Ceremony

Please silence and put away phones and cameras so everyone can be fully present. Professional photos will be shared afterward.

Rooftop Comfort

Expect warm weather, sun, and a breeze. Bring sunglasses, sunscreen, and a light layer for later in the evening.

Need directions? Ask the concierge or any resort staff member for “Armin and Christina’s wedding at the Sky Rooftop Gazebo.”

Useful While You’re Here

Guest Essentials
  • World of Hyatt app: Check restaurant hours, menus, daily activities, entertainment, spa information, and resort updates.
  • Concierge: Dial 0 from your room or visit the lobby for dining, spa, directions, or wedding-location help.
  • Evening dining: Keep one resort-elegant outfit ready for à la carte restaurants.
  • Small cash: The stay is all-inclusive, but small bills are useful for optional gratuities.
  • August weather: Expect heat, humidity, strong sun, and occasional brief showers. Carry sunscreen and a light layer.
  • Same-day information: Resort hours and access rules can change, so the concierge and app are the best sources during the stay.
