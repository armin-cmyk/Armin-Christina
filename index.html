CEREMONY DETAILS

Sky Rooftop Gazebo
Location

The Sky Rooftop Gazebo is on the 10th floor, at the highest point of the resort, with panoramic Caribbean views.

How to Get There

From the main lobby, take the elevators to the 10th floor and follow signs to the Sky Rooftop Gazebo near Sky Salon. From the pool or beach, ask any staff member to direct you to the rooftop elevators.

Allow 5 to 10 minutes from most areas. Please arrive by 4:50 PM.

An Unplugged Ceremony

Please silence and put away phones and cameras during the ceremony so everyone can be fully present. We will share the professional photos afterward.

Rooftop Tips
  • •It may be sunny, hot, humid, and breezy. Sunscreen and sunglasses are recommended.
  • •Brief summer showers are possible.
  • •Wear comfortable shoes for walking and dancing.
